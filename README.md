Hello friend, this is Martin.
====================
I’m just a tech, who like to code.
--------------------------------

I lived in Australia for 6 years and that’s the place where this all started. 

Starting off as a Freelance Web Developer and a little Programmer using C# .NET to develop and deploy small Window Application, over the year I’ve expanded my skills from Backend, Full Stack, Web App, Mobile Application and the recent years working a lot with Web3 and Blockchain.

Overall I’m also not just doing coding, I’ve also spend a lot of time into Networking, Cloud, Cyber Security, CI/CD DevOps and IoT. Big Data and Artificial Intelligent are the topic I want to explore more in the near future.

What I believe:

* There’s only the right tool for the right case, be more open minded
* Believe your instinct when you have doubt, often it’s correct
* The amount of time you invest is directly proportional to the outcome
* Quality over Quantity
* There’s so much to learn, so don’t waste your time
